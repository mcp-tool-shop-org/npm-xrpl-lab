Welcome to the XRPL Lab handbook. This is the complete guide to the hands-on XRPL training workbook that teaches blockchain literacy through verifiable artifacts.

XRPL Lab runs 12 modules across three progressive tracks -- Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. Every module teaches one skill and produces one verifiable artifact. No Python required -- it runs as a single binary via npm.
